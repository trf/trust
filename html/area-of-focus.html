WHAT WE DO

Our work is heavily focused on supporting more inclusive economies around the world. Our goal is to help create a positive environment in which social enterprises and purpose-driven companies can thrive and the private sector can act more responsibly, becoming accountable not only to shareholders but also to stakeholders, and society at large.

KEY INITIATIVES

Banks Alliance Against
Trafficking

Recognising the powerful contribution the financial sector can make to the anti-trafficking movement, we created the Banks Alliance Against Trafficking – a series of regional multi-stakeholder working groups that support financial institutions to fight human trafficking using their data.

Banks Alliance Against
Trafficking

Recognising the powerful contribution the financial sector can make to the anti-trafficking movement, we created the Banks Alliance Against Trafficking – a series of regional multi-stakeholder working groups that support financial institutions to fight human trafficking using their data.

LEARN MORE

Through TrustLaw, we provide more than 1,000 social enterprises in 80+ countries with free legal advice from top law firms, workshops, tools and resources – including guidance on navigating regulatory frameworks, accessing crowdfunding, registering in various jurisdictions, and training of professional service providers on how to reframe their advice to take into account the needs of inclusive economies.
